- **Skippy the Flying Squirrel:** Skippy has soft, fluffy fur of warm chestnut color, with patches of creamy white on his belly. His eyes are large, bright, and filled with curiosity, shimmering like little gems. The flaps of skin between his limbs are delicate and resemble tiny, translucent wings. His tail is bushy and expressive, often held high with excitement.

- **Oliver the Owl:** Oliver is an aged owl with feathers in a mix of silvery grays and deep browns, signifying wisdom. His eyes are penetrating and wise, glowing like amber beads, and he carries an air of ancient knowledge about him. Oliver's wings are broad and strong, allowing him to glide silently through the forest.
